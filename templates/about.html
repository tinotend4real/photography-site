The Philosophy

How I See It

👁️

Find the Angle

The obvious shot is never the best shot. I move, I wait, I crouch, I climb — whatever it takes to find the frame that makes people stop scrolling.

⚡

Chase the Light

Good light changes everything. I've waited forty minutes for a cloud to move. Light is the real subject.

🎯

Keep it Real

No fake poses. No heavy filters. Real moments, honest photography — that's what lasts.
